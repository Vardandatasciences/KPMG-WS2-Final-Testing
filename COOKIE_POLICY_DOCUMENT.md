## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Advanced Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) Platform ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. This policy should be read in conjunction with our Privacy Policy.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies allow a website to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions. This helps us provide you with a better experience when you browse our website and allows us to improve our services.

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:
- **Enhance your browsing experience** by remembering your preferences and settings
- **Analyze site traffic** to understand how visitors interact with our website
- **Personalize content** based on your preferences and usage patterns
- **Ensure website security** and prevent fraudulent activity
- **Improve our services** by understanding user behavior and preferences

The information collected through cookies helps us understand how visitors interact with our website and allows us to improve our services and user experience.

## Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize cookies into four main types based on their purpose:

### 1. Essential Cookies

**Status:** Always Active (Required)

**Purpose:** These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms.

**What they do:**
- Maintain your session while you navigate the website
- Remember your login status and authentication
- Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

- Enable core website functionality

**Examples:**
- Session management cookies
- Authentication tokens
- Security features
- Cookie consent preferences

**Can you disable them?** No. These cookies are essential for the website to function. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not work if you do so.

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest (necessary for the service)

## Cookie Retention

Cookies are stored on your device for different periods depending on their type:

### Session Cookies
- **Duration:** Temporary - deleted when you close your browser
- **Purpose:** Maintain your session while browsing
- **Examples:** Authentication tokens, session identifiers

### Persistent Cookies
- **Duration:** Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
- **Purpose:** Remember your preferences across sessions
- **Examples:** Language preferences, cookie consent choices, user settings

**Typical Retention Periods:**
- **Essential Cookies:** Session duration or up to 1 year
- **Functional Cookies:** Up to 1 year
- **Analytics Cookies:** Up to 2 years
- **Marketing Cookies:** Up to 2 years

You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

### Option 1: Cookie Banner (Recommended)
When you first visit our website, a cookie banner will appear at the bottom of the page. You can:
- **Accept All:** Enable all cookie types
- **Reject All:** Only enable essential cookies
- **Customize:** Choose which cookie types to enable

### Option 2: Cookie Preferences Page
You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by:
1. Clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link (if available in the footer)
2. Visiting our Cookie Policy page and clicking "Manage Cookie Preferences"
3. Accessing the cookie banner again (if you've previously dismissed it)

### Option 3: Browser Settings
Most browsers allow you to:
- **View cookies:** See what cookies are stored on your device
- **Delete cookies:** Remove cookies from your device
- **Block cookies:** Prevent websites from setting cookies
- **Allow specific cookies:** Choose which websites can set cookies

**Browser-specific instructions:**
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies and site permissions
- **Opera:** Settings → Privacy & security → Cookies

**Note:** Blocking or deleting cookies may impact your experience on our website. Some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

## Glossary

**Cookie:** A small text file stored on your device by a website.

**Session Cookie:** A temporary cookie that is deleted when you close your browser.

**Persistent Cookie:** A cookie that remains on your device for a set period or until you delete it.

**First-Party Cookie:** A cookie set by the website you are visiting.

**Third-Party Cookie:** A cookie set by a domain other than the one you are visiting.

**Local Storage:** A browser feature that allows websites to store data on your device (similar to cookies but with more storage capacity).

**Session Storage:** A browser feature similar to local storage but data is cleared when the browser tab is closed.
